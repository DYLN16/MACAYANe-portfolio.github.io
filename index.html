ADVANCED PROGRAMMING & WEB TECHNOLOGY

OCT 10, 2021

- This is the first seminar conducted for this subject. The speaker is very knowledgable of the topic discussed considering that their webinar topic is very broad and has a lot of areas to cover. And after attending this seminar I have a deeper understanding of different programming languages and web technology including front-end and back-end areas.

CURRENT TRENDS IN THE IT INDUSTRY

OCT 10, 2021

- This webinar focuses on the current trends in the industry of informational technology. We have learned lots of things like the importance of trends in the society, the difference of a virtual reality and augmented realty, Artificial Intelligence and many more.

IP MODULAR COURSE

OCT 18-20, 2021

- This seminar is conducted by our beloved subject adviser DR. Belinda Bunag together with MR. Gerald Jugo and ENGR. James Imperial. It is a 3 day seminar with different topics per day. On day one we focused on the Introduction to IP System, Copyright, Trademark and Patent. On the second and last day they discussed Prior Arts Search and Patent Information Search. This seminar is very important for us computer students since innovations of future technologies will be reluctant to future graduates of computer courses, having the proper background knowledge in patenting is important.

STORAGE & CLOUD COMPUTING

OCT 24, 2021

- The speaker of this seminar is our professor in our subject "Emerging technologies". This seminar helped us have a deeper understanding of how cloud computing works. This topic is very important since cloud storage and computing is very important in organizations and companies.

DATA SCIENCE

NOV 7, 2021

- This seminar is very engaging and fun since the speaker delivered the lessons in a whimsical manner by using the theme of pokemon. The part that really caught my attention is when the speaker discussed the skill sets required for being a data scientist. This includes Programming & Database, Domain knowledge & Soft Skills, Communication & Visualization, and Math & Statistics.

WIRELESS NETWORKS AND COMMUNICATION

NOV 14, 2021

- in this seminar we had a deeper understanding of wireless connections. We have tackled different areas of network communication including IP address or Internet Protocol. Having a deeper understanding is important for everyone since wireless communication have enabled the connection of billions of people to the Internet so that they can reap the benefits of today's digital economy.

BEST PRACTICES in IT INDUSTRIES

NOV 21, 2021

- For me this seminar entitled BEST PRACTICES in IT INDUSTRIES is the best and the most important seminar conducted for this subject. Having the proper knowledge about the best practices in IT undustries is very important for us computer students to prepare us on our future once we stepped in the corporate world.

CYBER SECURITY & DATA PRIVACY

NOV 28, 2021

- The seminar of CYBER SECURITY & DATA PRIVACY is also very important. having the proper knowledge of how to secure your private informations is important for your personal security. Other than that knowing how to properly secure data will help you for your future career.

LATEST TECHNOLOGY in EMBEDDED SYSTEM

DEC 5, 2021

- This seminar entitled LATEST TECHNOLOGY IN EMBEDDED SYSTEM is conducted by our group. The topic of this seminar is basically a general discussion of the previous lessons. We tackled Artificial Intelligence, Augmented and Virtual reality, Deep Learning, Embedded security, and Cloud Connect. This seminar also introduced us to embedded systems. That almost all of the current technologies is implemented with embedded systems.

NANOTECHNOLOGY

DEC 12, 2021

- This Seminer is very interesting because personally, I find nanotech very interesting. The speaker of this seminar is very knowledgable of the field and he managed to discuss the topic very clearly. The part of the seminar that caught my attention is the discussion of the influence of nanotech in our daily lives and apparently, nanotech influences our communication the highest among other areas of our lives.

COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE & ORGANIZATION

DEC 12, 2021

- In this seminar entitled COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE & ORGANIZATION, we have answered the question "what is a computer organization?" that it is the forerunners to modern computers attempted to assemble the raw devices (mechanical, electrical, or electronic) into a separate purpose-build machine for each desired behavior. We have also tackled the Memory Size or Speed of a computer, the operations on a Memory, Input/Output Subsystem and many more topics under the topic of a Computer Architecture.
